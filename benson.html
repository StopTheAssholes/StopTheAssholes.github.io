Some Reading:

In case you're eager to get started on understanding some of the facts around an oil magnate becoming CU president, here is some cited information to get you going:



  1. Prior to being CU President, Bruce Benson was a primary funder in Trailhead Group LLC, whose goals were to smear Democratic candidates in Colorado .
  2. During his appointment review, the faculty voted 40-4 against his appointment.
  3. After pressures to divest from fossil fuels since it didn't fit our campus values, Benson decided to just say 'no'. Further, in a university committed to research and science he presides over us as a climate change denier.
  4. Don't take it from us, see the memo a CU professor passed around years ago warning people of Benson and his intentions.
