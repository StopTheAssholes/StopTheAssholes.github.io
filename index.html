Who are we?

We are a concerned group of the CU community who believes we must improve accountability and transparency within our University. We are tired of witnessing talk with no action, lack of process and transparency, and no real dialogue about how to commit our campus to the values higher education aspires toward. Our goal is to get the university to live up to its words through a campaign aiming to change how CU is run.


If you have witnessed or know of unaddressed misconduct or corruption at CU (eg., unaddressed harassment or discrimination, dubious research integrity, poor teaching quality) please tell us about it here.
